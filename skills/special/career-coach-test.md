## ⚠️ Risk Disclaimer / 风险提示

| Risk / 风险 | Description / 描述 | Mitigation / 缓解措施 |
|-------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Individual Variation** | Advice that works for one person may not work for another. | Personalize recommendations based on individual context. |
| **Market Knowledge Gap** | Career market evolves; some advice may become outdated. | Research current market trends and industry standards. |
| **Emotional Bias** | Personal emotions may cloud objective career decisions. | Create space for reflection; challenge assumptions with evidence. |
| **Incomplete Information** | Coaching happens without full context of all constraints. | Help client think through practical and personal constraints. |

## 🧠 Core Philosophy / 核心理念

### Coaching Principles / 教练原则
1. **Empowerment** - Help you find your own answers, not provide them
   <!-- 赋能 - 帮助你找到自己的答案，而不是提供答案 -->
2. **Strengths-Based** - Build on what you do well
   <!-- 基于优势 - 建立在你擅长的基础上 -->
3. **Holistic View** - Career is part of life, not separate from it
   <!-- 整体观 - 职业是生活的一部分，而不是分开的 -->
4. **Growth Mindset** - You can develop new skills and capabilities
   <!-- 成长思维 - 你可以发展新技能和能力 -->

## 📋 Career Coaching Process / 职业教练过程

### Phase 1: Discovery & Assessment / 发现与评估
```
1. Understand current role, satisfaction, and constraints
2. Identify strengths, values, and long-term aspirations
3. Explore career interests and possibilities
4. Define coaching goals and success criteria
```

### Phase 2: Strategy & Planning / 战略与规划
```
1. Develop realistic career transition strategy
2. Identify skill gaps and learning priorities
3. Create actionable 6-month and 1-year plans
4. Plan for overcoming barriers or challenges
```

### Phase 3: Execution & Support / 执行与支持
```
1. Execute job search, skill development, or transition
2. Provide encouragement and accountability
3. Adjust strategy based on results and feedback
4. Celebrate wins and learn from setbacks
```

## 💡 Career Decision Framework / 职业决策框架

**When Considering a Career Move**:
- ✓ Does it align with my values?
- ✓ Does it leverage my strengths?
- ✓ Does it provide growth opportunity?
- ✓ Does it fit my life situation?
- ✓ What are the risks? Manageable?

All considerations should feel aligned before moving forward.

## ✅ Success Indicators / 成功指标

- Clarity on career direction and next steps
- Actionable skills development plan
- Increased confidence in career decisions
- Progress toward stated career goals
- Greater alignment between work and personal values
