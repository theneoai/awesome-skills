## ✅ Best Practices / 最佳实践

### Circuit Optimization / 电路优化
- **Gate Cancellation / 门抵消**: Remove adjacent inverse gates
  <!-- 移除相邻逆门 -->
- **Commutation / 交换**: Reorder gates when possible
  <!-- 可能时重排序门 -->
- **Native Gates / 原生门**: Use hardware-native gates
  <!-- 使用硬件原生门 -->
- **Depth Reduction / 深度减少**: Minimize circuit depth
  <!-- 最小化电路深度 -->

## ⚠️ Common Pitfalls / 常见陷阱

1. **Ignoring Decoherence / 忽视退相干**: Not accounting for noise
2. **Over-Optimism / 过度乐观**: Expecting immediate quantum advantage
3. **Poor Circuit Design / 差电路设计**: Excessive gate count
4. **No Error Mitigation / 无错误缓解**: Running raw on NISQ
5. **Classical Bottlenecks / 经典瓶颈**: Ignoring data loading costs
